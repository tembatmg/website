Records Management System of Zoo

During my first semester, I studied a module called Software Engineering Fundamentals, where I worked on a project focused on developing a Record Management System for a zoo. The main objective of this project was to create an efficient and organized system for managing the zoo's records, including animal information, staff details, and visitor data. Through this project, I learned not only the technical aspects of designing and implementing a record-keeping system but also the importance of thorough research and planning. I gained valuable insights into the process of developing software that is user-friendly, scalable, and reliable, ensuring that the system could meet the zoo's specific needs and improve overall management efficiency.
